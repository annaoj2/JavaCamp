Receipt
Uses: variable types, functions

0. Create a double and use it to print a receipt that has a price. 
E.g. 
Receipt: $10.27

1. Write a function called printReceipt that takes a price as an argument (parameter) and prints a "receipt" with the price. (Hint: use refactor)

2. Extend your function to also take the name of the item as an argument and print both the item and the price. 

3. Extend your function to also take the name of the person who bought it and say at the end of the receipt: 
"Thanks for shopping, NAME. Have a great day!"

5. Extend printReceipt to also take a quantity of an item. Multiply quantity by price per item to get the total price.
